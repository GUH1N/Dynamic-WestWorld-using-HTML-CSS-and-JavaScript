ABOUT WESTWORLD

Westworld is a vast and meticulously crafted theme park that transports visitors to a detailed recreation of the American Old West. The main town, Sweetwater, serves as the hub of the park, featuring a saloon, general store, train station, and other typical frontier establishments. There are also more remote areas like Escalante, as well as expansive wilderness landscapes that include forests, canyons, and plains. The park is populated by lifelike androids known as hosts, who interact with guests to create a fully immersive experience. Guests can engage in a variety of activities, such as gunfights, treasure hunts, and exploration, within a safe and well-maintained environment that combines historical authenticity with advanced technology.
