The Raj

The Raj is a colonial India-themed park designed for guests to indulge in exotic adventures and hunting expeditions.

Medieval World

"Medieval World," is a fictional setting where guests can immerse themselves in the chivalry, combat, and courtly life of the medieval era.

Shogunworld

Shogunworld is a Japanese-themed park that immerses guests in the Edo period, offering a world of samurai, ninjas, and geishas, designed for those seeking more intense and visceral experiences.
